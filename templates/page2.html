Metal

Metal is about raw emotion, energy, and rebellion. These bands bring intensity and influence to the genre:

Cannibal Corpse

Members: George "Corpsegrinder" Fisher, Alex Webster, Rob Barrett, Erik Rutan, Paul Mazurkiewicz
Notable: Formed in 1988, they're pioneers of death metal, infamous for graphic lyrics and brutal riffs. Still active and heavier than ever.

Listen on Spotify
Meshuggah

Members: Jens Kidman, Fredrik Thordendal, Mårten Hagström, Dick Lövgren, Tomas Haake
Notable: Known for polyrhythms and pioneering djent. Albums like ObZen and Nothing reshaped modern metal’s sound.

Listen on Spotify
Paleface Swiss

Members: Zelli (vocals), Marc (drums), Yannick (guitar), Roger (bass)
Notable: Hardcore beatdown from Switzerland. Their song “The Orphan” blew up in the underground scene for its sheer brutality.

Listen on Spotify
